Winter Solstice Spell Jar

From episode 7: Urgency is a Myth

Winter Solstice is the day with the fewest daylight hours, and marks the turn of the year towards the return of the light. It’s a natural ending/beginning day: a good time to let go of what no longer serves us, and turn towards what comes next. This practice is really fun to do as a seasonal celebration with other people; it’s also powerful intention-setting to do on your own. Regardless of your style of practice, it's very grounding and very pretty!

Created by Shanalee Hampton and is recorded here with her permission. The ritual was originally mentioned in Episode 5, but we included it in the show notes of Episode 7.
